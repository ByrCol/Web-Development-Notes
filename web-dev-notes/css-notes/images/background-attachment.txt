background-attachment sets wheter a background-images position is fixed within the viewport or scrolls with its containing block
/* Keyword values */
background-attachment: scroll;
background is fixed relative to the element itself and does not scroll with its contents
scroll works the opposite to what you would think
It "scrolls" with your scroll, it moves with the page
scroll is the normal effect essentially

background-attachment: fixed;
background is fixed to the viewport
background does not move with the element 
fixed is used for parallax effects

background-attachment: local;
background is fixed relative to the elements contents
background scrolls with the elements contents
